Particle Measuring Systems (USA) specializes in viable and nonviable particle counters that measure and monitor contamination levels in clean and controlled environments. Since 1972, their technology has been providing accurate and reliable results and information for our clients, PMS is one of the world's leading companies and manufacturers for particle counting instruments, and molecular and microbial monitoring. Technology Particle Measuring Systems provides in Bangladesh are: Particle Counters for Air and Gas, Liquid Particle Counters, Microbial Viable Contamination Monitors, Molecular Contamination Monitors & Facility Monitoring Systems.

They provide the best value for money lab instruments in Bangladesh including: Dissolution Testers (Offline + Online), Dissolution Media Preparator, Disintegration Testers, Friability Testers, Tablet Testers, Particle Size Analyzer, Powder Flow Tester, Tap Density Tester, Peristaltic Pumps, Leak Tester, Bulk Density Tester.
Malvern Panalytical UK technologies are used by scientists and engineers in a wide range of industries and organizations worldwide to solve the challenges associated with maximizing productivity, developing better quality products and getting them to market faster. In Bangladesh they are the leading providers of Particle Size Analyzers, Spray Particle and Spray Droplet Size Measurement Systems, Automated imaging for advanced particle characterization, Nanoparticle, Colloid and protein size and charge measurement systems.
Anton Paar Austria develops, produces and distributes highly accurate laboratory instruments as well as process measuring systems and provides custom-tailored automation and robotics solutions worldwide. They add value to customers by selectively acquiring other companies which helps them expand their product portfolio and continuously provide comprehensive solutions. Their supply in Bangladesh consists of a large portfolio including Density Meters, Flash Point Testers, Microwave Digestion Systems, Polarimeters, Raman Spectrometers, Rheometers, Viscometers and lots more.
ThermoFisher Scientific is an American biotechnology product development company located in Waltham, Massachusetts, and was created in 2006 by the merger of Thermo Electron and Fisher Scientific. In Bangladesh, their portable analytical instruments deliver instant, actionable data for quick decision making in critical situations.
