Glatt GmbH is the Pioneer in fluidized bed technology and offers a unique portfolio of innovative products and extensive services to support your company across the entire process chain, including process development for new products and formulations, process technology, planning, right up to construction of the plant itself to manufacture your product.
Romaco Group is a leading global manufacturer of packing and process technology. The company develops, manufactures and markets intelligent system solutions that are specific to the requirements of customers from the pharmaceutical industry. The portfolio includes tablet presses, blister and strip packaging machines, tube filling machines for effervescent tablets, cartoners, end of line equipment, liquid filling and powder dosing machines.

Since 1957 Brevetti C.E.A. has been confirming its ability to hit the mark by manufacturing ground-breaking inspection machines for injectable pharmaceutical products (Ampoules, Vials, Cartridges, LVP, Pre-filled Syringes). Across the world, Brevetti Cea references include a large variety of countries, customers and major international pharmaceutical groups. With more than 2,000 machines installed worldwide Brevetti Cea is internationally recognised as the inspection specialist.

Wherever pharmaceutical products are filled under the strictest hygienic conditions, special and custom-made machines by PLUMAT GmbH are the preferred choice. Continuously grown in all areas, the PLUMAT Group accompanies their customers as a development partner and system provider for pharmaceutical packaging systems, from the initial idea to the final product. Their solutions include FORM FILL SEAL (FFS) line for I.V Infusion, Bottle Filling Line for Parental Nutritional Product (i.e. Amino acid, fat emulsion etc), Stress Blow Moulding & I.V Infusion Filling Line for Pet Bottle.
Pamasol is Swiss Manufacturer which has been on the scene since the birth of the aerosol industry and meets all aerosol and spray system needs. Their systems include feeding, product filling, valve insertion, can sealing, propellant filling, spray head fitting, cap fitting etc. They have been advising customers from a wide range of industries for decades, which is why they understand customer's requirements precisely and provides them with support, following commissioning with quick service and expert advice.
ROTA Germany provides individual as well as integrated lines for ampoules, vials and bottles where essential process steps of washing, sterilizing, filling and closing are performed.

Weiler Engineering, Inc. is America's leading provider of aseptic custom packaging for pharmaceutical and healthcare applications. Continuous innovation and incorporation of the latest technological advances in equipment design and systems assure our customers of the highest level of quality in the production of sterile liquid products. Weiler Engineering, Inc.'s focus is to provide the most advanced Aseptic Liquid Processing technology available through the application of customized ASEP-TECH Blow/Fill/Seal machinery and integrated services.
SARONG Spa, established in 1972 in Italy, is the market leader in thermoforming fill and seal for SINGLE SERVE PACK machines (FFS) and the relevant packaging materials. To date, Sarong delivered more than 800 machines worldwide for pharmaceutical suppository and liquid unit doses & has been supplying FORM FILL SEAL (FFS) machines for unit dose packing in food, personal care, & chemical, since 1999.
The name SERVOLIFT has stood for innovation, flexibility and quality since 1976. SERVOLIFT offers complete transport, handling, cleaning and mixing technologies for all areas in pharmaceuticals, chemistry and food industries. The development and manufacturing of blenders, lifters, mobile devices, pharmaceutical containers, cleaning systems and special solutions reated for customer-specific requirements are the main areas of focus.
Steriline is an European manufacturer highly specialized in the production of complete lines for the aseptic processing of injectable products, supplying pharmaceutical companies worldwide. As a single-source provider of equipment solutions for the primary packaging of injectable drugs, Steriline develops, manufactures and supplies complete lines for the aseptic processing of toxic or non-toxic products, liquid or powders, in vials, ampoules, cartridges or syringes, including both mechanic and robotic applications.
With over 50 years of experience and an international presence in more than 100 countries, AzbilTelstar Life Science Solutions has focused its business in the field of Life Science product offerings which include GMP lyophilizers, equipment for contamination control and production of water for injection and pure steam, moist heat autoclaves, dry heat ovens, water stills, pure steam generators, industrial down-flow booths, containment isolators, bio-safety cabinets, containment and sterile isolators and ultrafreezers.
As a 100 % subsidiary of WIPOTEC, one of the world's leading businesses for dynamic weighing technology and product control, WIPOTEC-OCS stands for outstanding manufacturing quality "Made in Germany", the highest ease of integration and profound sector expertise. Their Checkweigher portfolio has the utmost accuracy at top speeds and therefore permits extremely high throughputs leading to an increasing line efficiency. Also catering to different pharma serialization requirements, they offer highly flexible Track and Trace systems which is reliable and future-proof solution for our pharmaceutical customers.
Volpak, a member of Coesia group is a leading manufacturer of horizontal form-fill-seal solutions for the pouch-type packaging in a wide range of market applications, from consumer goods to chemicals and pharmaceutical products. The company is built on robust technological foundations and can count on the expertise of more than 300 employees with the majority based at the Barcelona headquarters, where the equipment is designed, manufactured and marketed.
Hapa is a proud member of Coesia, a group of innovation-based industrial solutions companies operating globally and headquartered in Bologna, Italy. Their focus is to empower customers to improve lean production with printing solutions that add value to packaging processes.
Norden is the world's leading supplier of high-performance tube filling systems that focuses exclusively on this area. Norden has built the capabilities to assist their customers in any kind of tube filling application, providing dedicated systems and constant innovation.
SKY Softgel & Pack Co., Ltd. is a professional manufacturer and exporter for Softgel Encapsulation, Multi Counting, Blister & Cartoning and Autobag pouch machine. They have their own manufacturing plant and R&D center in South Korea where all machineries are manufactured on a Turn Key basis. They aim to supply the world with newly patented and advanced machinery with the most competitive price, highest quality and service.
For over 35 years Adam Fabriwerk has specialized in customized process system solutions for Pharmaceutical and Bio-pharmaceutical industries. They deliver customized turnkey solutions in Bangladesh for: Aseptic mixing and blending, Ointment and Cream, manufacturing, Liquid Oral manufacturing.
Truking Technology Limited was founded in 2000 and became one of the leading enterprises in pharmaceutical & medical equipment industry in the world after 17 years of development. In April 2017, it spent appox RMB 1.1 billion to acquire Romaco, Germany, the famous global manufacturer of packing and process technology, which expanded Truking's technological expertise and now possesses the largest output and plant scale in the world. Their product portfolio includes Overall Solution for Fluid Processing System, Sterile Products Lyophilization Production, Vial Injectable Products Production, Ampoule Injectable Products Production, Pre-Filling & Sealing Products Production and lots more.

The italian Company works worldwide by offering a customized approach to any specific requirement, to carry out tailor-made solutions for innovative projects of both Big Pharma companies and small galenic laboratories.

Products include high cycle rate standalone machines as well as complete turnkey solutions for food and pharmaceutical products, with overlap into cosmetic and chemical fields.
